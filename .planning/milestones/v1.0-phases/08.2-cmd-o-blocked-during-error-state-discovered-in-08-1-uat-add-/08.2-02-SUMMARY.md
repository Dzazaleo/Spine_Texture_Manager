## Threat model â€” mitigations applied

- **T-08.2-02-01** (Tampering: stale `mainWindow` after window closes): mitigated by mandatory `mainWindow?.webContents.send(...)` optional-chain in every menu click handler. The `mainWindow.on('closed', â€¦)` listener in `createWindow` nulls `mainWindowRef`, and the optional-chain makes the click a silent no-op rather than a `TypeError`. Identity check (`mainWindowRef === mainWindow`) guards against future races where a second window opens before the first window's `closed` fires.
- **T-08.2-02-02** (Spoofing: malicious `recent.json` pushes traversal payload through `'menu:open-recent-clicked'`): mitigated end-to-end by Plan 01's `validateRecentFile` (rejects non-string entries) combined with Plan 04's eventual routing through `window.api.openProjectFromPath(path)` â€” an existing channel that runs the project-io path-traversal + `.stmproj` extension validation (Phase 8 D-145). Defense-in-depth across two trust boundaries (recent.json validator + project-io path-extension check) per the threat register.
- **T-08.2-02-03** (Information Disclosure: Open Recent toolTip exposes full absolute paths): accepted per the threat register â€” same exposure as macOS NSDocumentController standard recent-files menu; user-chosen workspace artifacts; no secret material.
- **T-08.2-02-04** (DoS: malformed 10MB recent.json): accepted per the threat register â€” Plan 01's `MAX_RECENT = 10` cap on write is the load-bearing guard; cap-after-load defense-in-depth deferred to Phase 9 polish.
- **T-08.2-02-05** (EoP: `role: 'viewMenu'` exposes DevTools in packaged builds): mitigated â€” Electron's role-based viewMenu auto-hides DevTools-related items when `app.isPackaged === true` per the documented role behaviour.
