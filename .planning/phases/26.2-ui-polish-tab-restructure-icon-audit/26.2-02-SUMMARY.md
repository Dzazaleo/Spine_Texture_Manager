## Decisions Made

- **D-07 implementation form (extract vs inline):** Chose extract to `src/renderer/src/components/icons/WarningTriangleIcon.tsx`. Rationale: structurally enforces D-06 single-source-of-truth across current sites AND future warning-triangle uses; eliminates the four-file copy-paste hazard; the `grep -rl 'M10 3 L17 16 H3 Z' src/renderer/src` gate now mechanically guarantees one source.
- **D-06a dot rendering:** Chose `<circle cx="10" cy="14.5" r="0.5" fill="currentColor" />` over zero-length stroke path. Rationale: explicit element-local fill renders deterministically at 16x16px in dark mode without depending on `strokeLinecap="round"` filling a zero-length path (browser-rendering edge case).
